About Us

Company Overview:

At Jalicom Interiors Kenya, we transform spaces into extraordinary living and working environments. With a passion for creativity and an unwavering commitment to quality, we bring innovative designs to life, tailored to meet the unique needs and aspirations of our clients. Established with a vision to redefine interior aesthetics, our team of dedicated professionals ensures every project is a masterpiece.

Vision:

To enhance peoples' quality of life through interior design and architecture.

Mission:

To be the most reliable, creative,innovative and cost efficient architectural and interior design firm in offering quality services while enhancing peoples' quality of life by creating creative architectural designs and functional, sustainable, beautiful, luxurious, environment and health friendly interior spaces.

Design Philosophy:

At Jalicom Interiors, we believe in the power of personalized design. Every space tells a unique story, and our designs are crafted to reflect the essence of our clients. We combine innovation with functionality, creating harmonious environments that stand the test of time.

Business concept:

Jalicom Interiors Kenya is an architectural and interior design firm whose essence is to provide architectural and interior design solutions in the construction industry. We specialize in architectural design, construction administration, interior design and renovations with a focus on residential buildings, from mid to luxury homes and rental apartments.

Architectural Services:

Free Concept Sketching:

Architectural Drawing
